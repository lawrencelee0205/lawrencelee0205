About Me

Hello, I'm Lee Keen Shen, a Python developer with a strong focus on web development. I build software both for fun and as a profession, and I'm passionate about creating innovative and user-friendly web applications. My expertise lies in back-end development using modern Python frameworks like Django and front-end development with ReactJs.

I had the opportunity to work as a data scientist during my internship at Huawei, where I gained valuable experience in working with data and developing analytical solutions. During this internship, I collaborated with a team of talented professionals, applying machine learning algorithms and statistical models to derive valuable insights from large datasets. This experience allowed me to strengthen my analytical and problem-solving skills while working on real-world data-driven projects.

JavaScript became my language of choice due to its versatility and wide adoption in web development. I have extensive experience in building responsive and interactive web applications, employing technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript frameworks.

Aside from web development, I have an interest in exploring emerging technologies and learning new programming languages. I believe in continuous learning and staying up-to-date with industry trends to deliver the best solutions. In my free time, I enjoy attending tech meetups, reading tech blogs, and working on personal projects to enhance my skills.

I am excited to contribute to meaningful projects, collaborate with talented individuals, and continue growing as a developer. If you have any opportunities or would like to connect, feel free to reach out. Let's build something amazing together!
